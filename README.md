## About

Computer Science undergraduate at the University of Indonesia building end-to-end AI systems across classification, prediction, retrieval, and recommendation tasks. My work leans toward production-grade AI: RAG pipelines, knowledge-graph retrieval, continual learning frameworks, multimodal agents, and ML systems that can be evaluated, deployed, and improved.

I have worked on AI research and engineering across industry, academia, and competitions, including production RAG for customer service, stock-market predictive analytics, egocentric video annotation for deep learning research, AI curriculum development, and national-level data science competitions.
